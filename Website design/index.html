About me

So who am I? I'm 23 years old and pursuing a PhD in Fluid Dynamics at the University of Leeds. Why? Before completing a masters in mathematics at the university of East Anglia I had a pashion for learning and this pashion grew as I completed my four years. The EPSRC sponsored CDT at Leeds gives students from different backgrounds a great way of working together to achieve a common goal. This nature was just one reason why I chose this CDT but to learn more you can visit my student page.
